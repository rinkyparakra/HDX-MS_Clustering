# Computational Structural Biology Portfolio

**Rinky Parakra, PhD**  
Structural proteomics | HDX-MS | Protein dynamics | Cryo-EM/cryo-ET transition | Python workflows

This portfolio highlights reproducible computational workflows at the interface of structural biology, mass spectrometry, and cryo-electron microscopy. My long-term goal is to combine experimental structural proteomics with computational image analysis and molecular modeling to study dynamic macromolecular assemblies.

## Research Focus

My research background is in protein biochemistry, HDX-MS, conformational dynamics, and macromolecular complex analysis. I am especially interested in:

- Protein conformational dynamics and allostery
- Viral protein–host complex interactions
- HDX-MS data analysis and structural mapping
- Integrative structural biology
- Cryo-EM/cryo-ET image analysis and subtomogram averaging
- Python-based reproducible data workflows

## Featured Projects

### 1. HDX-MS Uptake Analysis Workflow
A Python workflow for analyzing peptide-level deuterium uptake, calculating differences between states, estimating significance, and preparing data for structural visualization.

**Skills shown:** Python, pandas, NumPy, matplotlib, statistical analysis, structural biology interpretation.

Folder: [`projects/01_hdx_ms_uptake_analysis`](projects/01_hdx_ms_uptake_analysis)

---

### 2. HDX-MS Structural Mapping with PyMOL
A template workflow for mapping peptide-level HDX-MS changes onto protein structures using PyMOL scripts and B-factor coloring.

**Skills shown:** PyMOL, protein structures, visualization, data-to-structure mapping.

Folder: [`projects/02_hdx_structural_mapping`](projects/02_hdx_structural_mapping)

---

### 3. Protein Dynamics Clustering
A notebook-style template for clustering peptide uptake behavior across time points and experimental states.

**Skills shown:** Python, clustering, dimensionality reduction, structural proteomics.

Folder: [`projects/03_protein_dynamics_clustering`](projects/03_protein_dynamics_clustering)

---

### 4. Cryo-EM Learning Track
A learning project documenting my transition into cryo-electron microscopy, including terminology, software tools, and planned public-dataset practice workflows.

**Skills shown:** cryo-EM concepts, tilt-series alignment, tomogram reconstruction, subtomogram averaging, computational learning plan.

Folder: [`projects/04_cryoET_learning_track`](projects/04_cryoET_learning_track)
