# Cryo-ET Terms

## Tilt Series
A set of 2D projection images collected at different tilt angles.

## Tomogram
A 3D reconstruction calculated from the tilt series.

## Fiducial Alignment
Alignment of tilt images using markers, often gold beads.

## Patch Tracking
Alignment method that uses image features rather than fiducials.

## CTF Correction
Correction for contrast transfer function effects introduced by the microscope optics.

## Subtomogram Averaging
Extraction, alignment, classification, and averaging of repeated particles from tomograms to improve signal and resolution.

## Missing Wedge
Anisotropic loss of information caused by the limited tilt range in cryo-ET.

## High-throughput Cryo-ET
Automated or semi-automated processing of many tilt series, tomograms, and subtomograms using scalable computing workflows.
